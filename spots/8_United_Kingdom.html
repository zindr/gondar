United Kingdom

London (2014-00):

Famous London is another world city, offering interesting museums that are often free. The mix of cultures results in great world kitchen.

Canterbury (1991-05):

The small city of Canterbury is often visited for its famous cathedral.

Cambridge (2009-08):

Cambridge is home to one of the most famous universities, which I visited as part of a congress. Related to that university are a few historical buildings. The town is ok, and is much more English than world city London. 

Edinburgh (2016-05):

Capital of Scotland, with its castle. It was ok, maybe a bit less than expected. Maybe it was the weather.

hike Meall a Bhuachaille (2016-05):

A first hike in the Cairngorms national park, alright but not that spectacular.

Kinlochewe (2016-05):

While the town is just a tiny tourist hub, getting there and away is really beautiful. Seemingly there are some very good hikes around.

Plockton (2016-05):

A tiny somewhat beautiful town on the road. While picturesque as promised, the scenery near is more to my taste.

Portree (2016-05):

This town is the living proof that you do not visit upnorth Scotland for its citylife.

Hike Old Man of Storr (2016-05):

Nice hike up the mountain with spectacular scenery, reminding me of the Lofoten in Norway a bit.

Hike Quiraing (2016-05):

Short hike to some stone formations we did not find. The scenery was again very good.

Hike Fairy Pools Glen Brittle (2016-05):

Short hike because it was near to the loosy camping ground (better camp wild)

Hike Bruach Na Frithe (2016-05):

Very good hike up the mountain, on top in the snow the ridges. Beautiful.

Fort William (2016-05):

The main street was a pleasant place to stretch the legs, good for an evening I guess.

Hike Steall Falls (2016-05):

Stretching the legs is also possible nearby Fort William, just a small hike up to the waterfall.

Hike Cobbler (2016-05):

An OK last hike in Scotland, nothing that brilliant though. The small cities nearby, no reason to visit that it seems.

Killin (2016-05):

This small town is picturesque, with its broad river, but that is about it. You could hike up the hill, we did not.
