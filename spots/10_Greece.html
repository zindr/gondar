Greece

Athens (1994-09):

Athens was not particularly interesting. It is home of the Acropolis, which I missed out on.

Delfi (1994-09):

The oracle of Delfi is known, and in town there are some ruins and a museum which are OK. 

Itea (1994-09):

Itea is a small coastal place near Delfi with boring shallow water to have a swim in.

Naflion (1994-09):

Naflion is a tourist city in the south of the Pelopponesos, handy because of its vicinity to various other places.

Tolo (1994-09):

Tolo is maybe the best place if you like swimming in the sea, here there are actually waves and sand.

Epidavros Theatre (1994-09):

The theatre was really interesting, especially observing how well the sounds made it from the centre all the way up. 

Mykene (1994-09):

While of historical importance, I thought this was just a bunch of old stones.

Mavromati (1994-09):

At the time this was just a tiny little village with some ruins that they were uncovering. I thoroughly enjoyed sitting there, drinking coffee with my girlfriend and watching how nothing seemed to happen.

Methoni (1994-09):

There is nothing of interest here, with a wave-less beach.

Sparta (1994-09):

Just another city in the Pelopponesos.

Olimpia (1994-09):

Here you can observe the track and some old building, fairly nice.

Ioannina (2014-06):

stopover, on route from Albania to the Meteora.

Kalambaka (2014-06):

The city of Kalambaka, with nearby tiny town Kastraki, are the gateway to the Meteora, beautiful landscape with monasteries on top of mountains.

Thessaloniki (2014-06):

The city of bars, a good vibe and just a nice place to spend a day or maybe more. There are some historical interesting sites to visit as well.
