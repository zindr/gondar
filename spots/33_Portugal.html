Portugal

Lisbon (2006-08):

I visited it because of a 7 day congress, meaning that I only was a tourist in the evenings.

Porto (2018-06):

Nice city, and fun because of the festivities for Saint John when people hit each-other with plastic hammers.

Lamego (2018-06):

A break in the road trip following the Douro, pleasant with its fort and excavations.

Braganca (2018-06):

The main city north east was an ok stop over.

Montesinho (2018-06):

A picturesque village that was the gateway to a fine 5 hour hike.

Moimenta (2018-06):

A small town that serves as a gateway to a fine 2.5 hour hike.

Campo Do Geres (2018-06):

A third hike that brought us along the old roman route to a bridge and refreshing pools.

Guimaraes (2018-06):

Charming city with a fortress, ideal to spent a last day to soak up the atmosphere.
