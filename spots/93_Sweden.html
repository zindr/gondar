Sweden

Stockholm (2015-08):

It is a beautiful city, green too, with some really nice areas to hang out, and you could visit the Vasa museum like everybody else.

road trip (2014-08):

just a stopover in Sweden, going from Denmark to Norway.

Nykoping (2015-08):

An ok city for a brief stop, ryan air flies to nearby Skavsta.

Abisko (2015-08):

This well established National Park offers a few nice day hikes, or even shorter, and is the start of various multi-day treks. We spent just a day.

Vassijaure (2015-08):

A few houses, university cottages and a station serve as one of the bases to hike to the Trollsjon lake.

Trollsjon (2015-08):

Go very early for a skinny dip in the ice cold water. The hike is nice.

Kiruna (2015-08):

Yearning for a bit of city, this is it in the north but do not expect too much though.

Sweden Lakes (2015-08):

On the road you cross various lakes, perfect for camping if it were not for these darn mosquitos. We stayed at sjon moskojarvi.

Gallivaer (2015-08):

There is not much reason to visit Gallivaer but if you happen to, it is not too bad either.
