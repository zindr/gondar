Albania

Tirana (2014-06):

The capital city has a nice vibe to it, maybe not showing many tourist attractions but definitely a must visit as it shows a bit the other side to Albania.

Shkoder (2014-06):

Not much of interest as a city in itself, but OK as a first experience of Albanian atmosphere.

Berat (2014-06):

Considered as a highlight in Albania, it offers a nice citadel/old town up the mountain and some nice alleys to walk around, good for a day.

Llogara NP (2014-06):

This national park offers a few hikes up mountain passes, if you find where they are. A 4 hour hike offered some very nice forest and eventually some beautiful views. A bit more south, the road goes through the beautiful and well known Llogara pass.

Vuno (2014-06):

Tiny little Vuno has been put on the map because of the Shkola hostel and the nearby gorge with its nice secluded beach.

Vuno Gorge (2014-06):

Walkable from Vuno, though olive plantations and shrubs, this secluded beach with its gorge if for many a highlight to Albania.

Saranda (2014-06):

on route, a big city with beach and hotels.

Gjirokaster (2014-06):

Known for its old town, this city is one of the tourist highlights and offers quite nice strolls up and down the old town hill. It offers a nice day out.
