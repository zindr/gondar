Lesotho

Butha Buthe (2016-10):

Passing through, after the border crossing. Note the the northern road is your only option with a 2wd to get to the Sani-Pass.

Katse Dam (2016-10):

An allright place to visit, but getting there is by far the most important part of the trip.

Maseru (2016-10):

It is a capital, but there is no real reason to visit unless you like the more dodgy places. Sleeping we did in Thaba Bosui.

Malealea (2016-10):

There is beautiful scenery around for hiking. The community based tourism, as they promote themselves, is maybe more about advertising yourself.

Qatchas Nek (2016-10):

Like most places in Lesotho, getting there is most of the fun.
