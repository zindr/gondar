Moldova

Chisinau (2010-05):

The weirdest capital in Europe, probably, it takes only a few minutes to get from the center to dirt roads and there are hundreds of money changers. Rich and poor mix. Weird place.

Trebujeni (Orheiul Vechi) (2010-05):

If there is one thing to do in Moldova aside from drinking wine, then it might be a visit to the monastery of Orheiul Vechi. But even that one is only of real interest for experts, I guess.

Tiraspol (2010-05):

The capital of the non country Transnystria is surely even more surreal than Chisinau. If you have the chance, come over here for a few hours and be amazed.
