Burkina Faso

Ouagadougou (2008-01):

An African city most people enjoy... fitting African stereotypes, slums in some parts and trying to be upmarket in other parts. Just hang out there would do for a couple of days. There was one budget option for sleeping, at walking distance from the airport.

Ouahigouya (2008-01):

stop over after crossing the border

Bobo-Dioulasso (2008-01):

Another very nice African town... with dusty roads and a lot of activity. There is a Sudanese built mosque and an interesting looking train station and market. And city life hear can offer an OK nights out.

Banfora (2008-01):

Maybe you do not want to hang out for too long. Nice people but small and nothing of interest in itself. The surroundings are very nice though.

Tengrela (2008-01):

Postcard moments on my way walking to tiny little Tengrela, really, superb nature, rural life, ... 

Po (2008-01):

Insignificant town on the road to Ghana. Some people make it to nearby Tiebele with its traditional houses and culture life.
