United Arab Emirates

Dubai (2014-01):

Plastic fantastic... be awed by the many skyscrapers and the biggest, the highest, the most... whatever. It is not a backpacking place. Shop, what else really is there to do ?

Sharjah (2014-02):

An easy day trip from Dubai, just for having a different taste of the region, but do not expect that there is much to see or do.

Abu Dhabi (2014-02):

The UAE capital, trying to catch up with Dubai in its growth. It partially owns Dubai. There is a mosque that is worth a visit. Again, no place to backpack.

Al Ain (2014-02):

The oasis town near the Oman border. It is boring for most but it has a few things you can visit if you get stuck on the road to or from Oman.
