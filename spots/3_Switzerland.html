Joriseen (2020-06):

A first hike up to 2770 meters, to have a look at the lake which was somewhat iced.

Casaccia hike (2020-06):

An up and down from town offered nice high mountain scenery, some lakes and woody slopes, keeping us busy for nearly 7 hours.

Tamboalp hike (2020-06):

An up and down from Splugen offered nice mountain scenery, with a view on the pass to Italy, keeping us busy for 5 hours. We camped in Italy, at the Montespluga lake.

Goscheneralp hike (2020-06):

A circular around a lake proved especially nice at the northern end, because of its boulders and vegetation,4 hour hiking worth the trip. The nearby Furka pass offers a superb road trip, beautiful scenery too.

Five Lakes trail (2020-06):

An up and down from Tasch, and Zermatt which is surprisingly big and has a few interesting looking streets, brought us to the 5 lakes trails, popular, but maybe the gondolas do take away a bit of the adventure. For us it was over 9 hours of hiking.

Grindelwald (2020-06):

A quick stop in this beautifully located city, surrounded by mountains, gave a hint of what we would miss out on, rain was coming.

Bern (2020-06):

A quick stop to get a glimpse of city life in Switzerland, Bern has a tiny old center that was very nice to stroll around in on a sunny afternoon. Of course, how delightful it was, Switzerland is for the hiking, the rest is too expensive.
