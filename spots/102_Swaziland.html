Swaziland

Nhlangano (2016-10):

The road up from this tiny city should be scenic, bummer it was raining cats and dogs.

Milwane Wildlife Sanctuary (2016-10):

This popular stopover provides cheap accomodation in the middle of a wildlife reserve. The hippo trail is a nice hike. Nearby Ezulwini, despite being recommended, seems to be a bit too boring.

Mbabane (2016-10):

A capital, not that there is much out there. It is good enough to have a walk through or to shop. To sleep there are probably better options.

Siteki (2016-10):

Cities in Swaziland you could hardly call cities, just like Siteki they look like a few malls with some people living near it.

Hlane NP (2016-10):

If you want to come up close to lions, this zoo-like national park may be for you, otherwise, give it a miss.

Malalotja (2016-10):

Maybe you see some animals on this hike, but you surely will enjoy the hilly hike, and maybe the outdoor camping in the canyon.
