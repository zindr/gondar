Romania

Bucarest (2012-06):

It is maybe not the nicest European capital but the center of the center is quite nice and there are a few places of interest.

Brasov (2012-06):

The tourist spot of the country, with its nice center and convenient for a few side trips.

Sinaia (Peles Castle) (2012-06):

The Peles Castle is quite remarkable, the only that I visited because it was considered best of the three. The town is not interesting in itself.

Zarnesti (2012-06):

The starting place for trips into the Piatra Craiului National Park, supposedly because I did not find the start of any hikes.

Sighisoara (2012-06):

Another small city, quite nice, with some castle ruins and a torture room on display.

Sighetu Marmatlei (2012-06):

Another place where there should be some hiking, but I did not find them. The area was nevertheless nice to walk through.

Oradea (2012-06):

The border city was actually quite nice, it was good for a few hours the first time, and worth comming back for a day the second.

Cluj Napoca (2024-08):

As many cities in Romania, it is small, beautifull, and ok to hang out in.

Sibiu (2024-08):

Known for its beauty, this small town is OK for a short while, and you can use it to enter the Fagaras mountains but mind the bears.

Deva (2024-08):

This small city has a citadel up the mountains that draws some tourists, I was there to get to the national park down south.

Retyezat NP (2024-08):

Some say this is the most beautiful national park, it was a very good two days hike going up to 2500m, with glacial lakes and great vistas.

Timisoara (2024-09):

This student city has some nice river side bars, and the beautiful squares you can expect in Romania.

Rodnei Mountains NP (Borsa) (2024-09):

Near Borsa in the Maramures region are nice hikes, one I did taking me 2 days with great views.
