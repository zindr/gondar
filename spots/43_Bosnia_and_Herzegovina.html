Bosnia and Herzegovina

Sarajevo (2008-10):

A beautiful city center with nearby clear evidence of the recent war. A 12.5 Euro tour brings you a bit closer to that war, with a look into the tunnels.

Kostajnica (2014-06):

Border crossings, not a place for visiting in itself.

Bosanska Krupa (2014-06):

on route, while hitch-hiking.

Sanski Most (2014-06):

Hitch-hiking could bring you to this nice little city. Not much to do though.

Jajce (2014-06):

One of the few tourist cities within Bosnia, it offers a waterfall and an old town with citadel. Maybe even this town had limited things on offer.

Mostar (2014-06):

This city is very well known, with its UNESCO bridge and old town. It is nice for having a walk around for a day.

Blagaj (2014-06):

Seemingly this is the camping area near Mostar, with a river and a few historic sites around.

Procitelj (2014-06):

Another historic site on route, with a fort to hike up and down for an hour.
