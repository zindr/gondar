Israel

Jerusalem (2022-09):

A city with history like few other, loved standing on temple mountain, and wandering the narrow streets, as well as the more modern part of town.

Mitzpe Ramon (2022-09):

Amidst the biggest crater, at the highest point, the scenery is very nice, in the Negev desert, I also did the Ramon's tooth loop, Makhtesh Ramon.

Metsada (2022-09):

After hitchhiking, and busses, through OK Dimona and touristic Ein Bokek, Masada is a treat, some of the history was still clear, it was nice.

Ramallah (2022-09):

Visiting Israel without visiting the West Bank would have been wrong, so I went for having a taste. Not that much is happening there, but I would not want to have missed it.

Nablus (2022-09):

This city is quite nice, with its walled city still a clear part intertwined with the new city. It is also a place where maybe the tension with Israel is a bit more clear.

Bethlehem (2022-09):

The old part of the city was again quite nice, and the wall is a vivid reminder of the current and past situation. You can visit the birthplace of Jesus, or that is what they say.

Tel Aviv (2022-09):

The old part of the city was again quite nice, and the rest is hip and trendy, cool places but expensive. And it is nice to have the beach.
