Japan

Tokyo (2013-04):

The city of cities in the region... it was a very nice introduction into Japanese city live... enough to keep your amazement going for a few days. I was very lucky with couchsurfing.

Yokosuka (2013-04):

Because I found a couchsurf host, no other reason to come here. But it was fun for the brief moment that I was there, a start-off point to visit nearby Karakuma with its temples.

Kamakura (2013-04):

If it is temples that you are after, some of them you will find here. For the rest it seems to be just another Japanese town.

Takayama (2013-04):

Another city of temples with a lot of old Japanese tourists.

Nagoya (2013-04):

Industrial Nagoya boast good food, I am told... but remains nothing but an industrial city to get in and get out again.

Inuyama (2013-04):

Here you can find a castle, I just wanted to find a ride out of town. I eventually found one, it was brilliant because Japanese people are very hospitable too.

Kyoto (2013-04):

The city of temples... and it is true, there are a lot spread around the city. Still, it is more than just temples... This city is quite nice.

Osaka (2013-04):

If you look for another Tokyo, this is it... here it gets as cosmopolitan as it gets in Japan. I liked it.
