Zambia

Lusaka (2010-12):

Yet another African capital... the dirty market is interesting, but do not go to the city center in the evening, there are better places for getting a drink near the hostels.

Kitwe (2010-12):

Way out of the tourist track, no tourists and no tourist infrastructure, but for me it ended up as one of the absolute highlights in travel, serving drinks at a real Zambian party with all my new friends.

Chingola (2010-12):

A fairly rich town, thanks to the mining industry... still dusty though. It gives a bit a different flavor than nearby Kitwe. Note that the Congo border is near here.

Kapiri Mposhi (2010-12):

transit... another dusty town, a transport hub to the north.

Serenje (2010-12):

Another dusty town, in transit... sleeping in the garden at the police office... African travel is interesting, even if there is nothing.

Mutinondo reserve (2010-12):

A reserve of a rich English guy, open for the public... nice for a brief stop with some forest and big boulders dotting the place, nice if you have a way to cross the 25 km dirt road.

Isoka (2010-12):

On transit, transport is not easy... another dusty town, it is always waiting and waiting, finally after many hours the truck moves... I could be in front, a sign of respect.

Muyombe (2010-12):

Getting stuck again, in the middle of nowhere... but this way it is fun, it is really a taste of real life.

Chama (2010-12):

And getting stuck again, trying to get from A to B... another dusty town, here I ate centipedes.

Lundazi (2010-12):

Almost there, a glimpse of civilization but not there yet.

Chipata (2010-12):

Crossing borders... and border towns... not the worst here!
