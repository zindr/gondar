Bulgaria

Sofia (2008-10):

This capital ok, but nothing more. I enjoyed going out here.

Plovdiv (2008-10):

Very nice center, and there are a few things you can visit but it will not take you long to get through.

Veliko Tarnuvo (2008-10):

Maybe the nicest city to visit here, perched on a hill. There is not very much to keep you busy though.

Rila Monastery (2008-10):

One of the tourist draws is this monastery, which really is a bit interesting.

Bansko (2022-09):

A ski resort in winter, very touristy, a hiker's base in summer... I did 2 day hikes, to the unsettling Koncheto Ridge and very popular Vihren Peak, and I also very much enjoyed a smaller trip to nearby lakes.

Varna (2022-09):

Rivaling Burgas, this city the the gateway from the black sea, OK enough to wander.

Slanchevo (2022-09):

Gateway to small Pobitite Kamani, the stone forest, is apparently a real attraction, not sure what to think of it.
