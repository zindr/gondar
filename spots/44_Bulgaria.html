Bulgaria

Sofia (2008-10):

This capital ok, but nothing more. I enjoyed going out here.

Plovdiv (2008-10):

Very nice center, and there are a few things you can visit but it will not take you long to get through.

Veliko Tarnuvo (2008-10):

Maybe the nicest city to visit here, perched on a hill. There is not very much to keep you busy though.

Rila Monastery (2008-10):

One of the tourist draws is this monastery, which really is a bit interesting.

Bansko (2008-10):

A ski resort in winter, there is maybe not enough to do in summer... maybe there are some hikes, in that area at least there are some.
