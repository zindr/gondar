Spain

Madrid (2008-08):

Another very nice city, with beautiful museums and nice atmosphere.

Barcelona (2008-08):

I love Barcelona, it has it all with the Ramblas, beach, Gaudi architecture, nice food, ... 

Oviedo (2008-08):

5 days for a congress, it was nice to have a different perspective on Spanish cities.

Granada (2015-12):

The Alhambra will be the primary reason for a visit, but the city is nice too and tapas are included with a drink.

Ronda (2015-12):

The small city of Ronda provides a fine break in between the nearby cities with its beautiful surroundings.

Sevilla (2015-12):

The major city in Andalucia is really worth a visit, known for its cathedral and boasting many tapas bars.
