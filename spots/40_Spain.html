Spain

Madrid (2008-08):

Another very nice city, with beautiful museums and nice atmosphere.

Barcelona (2008-08):

I love Barcelona, it has it all with the Ramblas, beach, Gaudi architecture, nice food, ...

Oviedo (2008-08):

5 days for a congress, it was nice to have a different perspective on Spanish cities.

Granada (2015-12):

There are two things to mention, the Al Hambra which is interesting and the tapas that are like small free meals.

Ronda (2015-12):

Small city life in Andalusia with a natural back-drop and a slice of history, quite alright for a brief visit.

Sevilla (2015-12):

Maybe everyone likes Sevilla, and lucky us we were there during the Spanish festivities and beautiful winter sun.
