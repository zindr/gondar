Costa Rica

San Jose (2010-03):

Looking modern at the main street but much rougher a bit further. It is again another slightly dodgy but interesting place in where there is not much to do.

Monteverde (2010-02):

A tourist heaven; hike superb cloud forest, slide the zipline through jungle, do a night safari to spot tarantulas. Join the drunk 20 year old tourist in the afternoon on the street... crap.

Upala (2010-02):

If you want to see what a real city without tourists would be like, this may do it... very friendly and boring.

Puerto Chimenez (2010-03):

This little town is nothing but a gateway to the nearby national park: Corcovado.

Corcovado National Park (2010-03):

The park is brilliant, you can do trips of 2 days or more to go from Carate to Sirena along the beach that touches the jungle and back along a different track right through the jungle. 

Hike corcovado NP (2010-03):

A very nice 2 day hike in and out of the jungle.
