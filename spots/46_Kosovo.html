Kosovo

Pristina (2008-10):

I loved my visit, it was not nice but a bit surreal to find it in Europe. It was not yet designed for any type of tourism.

Prizren (2008-10):

This lovely town with its charming bridge and nice people was great for a half day side trip.

Peja (2018-08):

At the foot of the accursed mountains, this is actually a nice city to hang out and have a beer, there is even a beach.

Accursed Mountains (2018-08):

A first day hiking towards Albania brings you from Kuqishte almost all the way to the border. It was a nice hike, fairly diverse and not too difficult.
