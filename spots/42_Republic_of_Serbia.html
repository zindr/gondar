Republic of Serbia

Belgrade (2008-10):

It is maybe a party city, a bit rough and dull looking at first but stuff happens. Maybe you can catch a 2 Euro ballet performance. Otherwise, I may be a bit to much passed my years of partying for this city.

Novi Sad (2008-10):

This student city has a superb main square and a fort that is OK to visit to keep you busy for a few hours. Actually, the second visit,10 years later, it was a fun night out, including a local festival and many bars.

Subotica (2008-10):

A charming city, when I was there filled with flowers. The tourist information office was really nice.

Nis (2008-10):

Nice for its people and the tourist information office, but lacking tourist attractions. The skull tower and concentration camp were both a bit disappointing.

Golubac (2018-08):

The impressive route along the Danube starts not far from the little fortress here.

Negotin (2018-08):

A lucky stopover for the night in this small but nice town, good for a drink and a nice room.

Studenica Monastary (2018-08):

A road trip includes long drives, passing by the monastery, ok if it is your thing.

Novi Pazar (2018-08):

We should have stayed a bit longer in this quirky city, it appears interesting enough to hang around for a day.

Nova Varos (2018-08):

Or we should have stayed a bit longer in this tourist city, with its lake nearby.

Uvac Meanders (2018-08):

With a detour we finaly reached the meanders, a perfect reason to stretch the legs.

Ivanjica (2018-08):

Road trips bring you to places you would not normally visit, althoug only briefly. It was fun.

Lazarevac (2018-08):

Road trips bring you to places you would not normally visit, althoug only a night. It was fun.
