Republic of Serbia

Belgrade (2008-10):

It is maybe a party city, a bit rough and dull looking at first but stuff happens. Maybe you can catch a 2 Euro ballet performance.

Novi Sad (2008-10):

This student city has a superb main square and a fort that is OK to visit to keep you busy for a few hours.

Subotica (2008-10):

A charming city, when I was there filled with flowers. The tourist information office was really nice.

Nis (2008-10):

Nice for its people and the tourist information office, but lacking tourist attractions. The skull tower and concentration camp were both a bit dissappointing.
