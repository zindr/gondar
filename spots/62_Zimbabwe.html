Zimbabwe

Harare (2010-12):

African capitals... what can I say, it is always a bit interesting but there is very little in terms of tourist attractions.

Bulawayo (2010-12):

A nice introduction to Zimbabwe city life, I enjoyed the centre and also a few places at the outskirts where I went with a local that I met.

Livingston (Victoria Falls) (2010-12):

The widest of all waterfalls in the world, world famous... a must see if you are around. Off course, the town is a bit overrun by tourists. Take the train out if you are in for an uncomfortable adventure.

Marondera (2010-12):

Smaller towns in Zimbabwe... interesting for a short visit, just to see the difference with the big cities.
