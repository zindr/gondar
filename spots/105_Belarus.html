Belarus

Minsk (2017-09):

Surprisingly stylish, the capital boasts the finest of Soviet architecture and even a bit of coziness. Lucky for me the weather turned to the best and all of Minsk was celebrating the 950th birthday.

Viciebsk (2017-09):

A lot smaller than Minsk but in itself very nice. It is a great alternative view on Belarussian city life.

Polatsk (2017-09):

It gets even smaller here, but for a few hours this town is perfect.

Brest (2017-09):

I may have spent just a bit too little time in attractive Brest to get the full experience, I enjoyed it.

Kamianiuki (2017-09):

If you do not speak the language, prepare yourself before going to the Bielaviezskaja Pusca national park, I think I kinda missed most of what is out there. I did do a small hike and saw the bison, caged.
