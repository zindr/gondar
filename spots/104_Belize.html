Belize

San Ignacio (2017-03):

One of the highlights if you believe the guidebooks, a tourist street and nearby a few touristy attractions. It was a good place to get started.

Mountain Pine Ridge (2017-03):

An easy gettaway from San Ignacio is this park, with an OK cave and a bit of nice water scenery.

Belize City (2017-03):

Once a capital city, the biggest city of the country was quite alright to hang around for a day.

Belmopan (2017-03):

More a joke than a capital city, probably the tiniest in the world, it takes only half an hour to get a sense of what it is like.

Hopkins (2017-03):

A popular backpacking place at the Atlantic, Garifuna style. It is a relaxed place but that seems to be it.

Placencia (2017-03):

The upmarket version of Hopkins, filled with tourists and rich people, but maybe also quite boring. Anyway, it was good for the three hours I was there.

Cockscomb Basin Forest Reserve (2017-03):

A beautiful stretch of forest, but without much animals unless you are very lucky to see the jaguar.

Jalacte (2017-03):

A border crossing like no other, through someones garden, and now I know that it is not official so you end up illegally in Guatemala.
