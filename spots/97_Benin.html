Benin

Porto Novo (2015-03):

The capital is much smaller and much more relaxed than Cotonou and alright for spending a few hours too.

Cotonou (2015-03):

While not suggested to be the nicest of places, it does offer a very interesting market and some opportunities to be amazed.

Ouidah (2015-03):

Ouidah is a quite nice city, big enough to be entertained and boasting the point of no return at the end of the slave route.

Abomey (2015-03):

Abomey may be the nicest city of Benin, with its history as the center of a huge kingdom of which you can visit some of the remains.

Ganvie (2015-03):

Probably the most touristy place in the neighborhood, the stilt villages of Ganvie are quite interesting albeit touristed.

Natitingou (2015-03):

The northern city that is used to visit the Pendjari National Park and the Somba region that is like the Tamberma in Togo is a small city like everything here.

Boukoumbe (2015-03):

The border town of Boukoumbe is a good place to enter Benin, with its share of the Tamberma culture to see.
