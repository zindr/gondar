Netherlands

Amsterdam (1990-00):

Wandering around in Amsterdam is really enjoyable. Unfortunately, the people are a bit rude. Hostels are very expensive.

Maastricht (1990-00):

Maastricht is one of the nicest Dutch cities, with a bit of history and a river cutting through it.

Sittard (1990-00):

Sittard is just small city, nothing of interest here.

Eindhoven (1990-00):

Eindhoven is not a tourist destination, it is much more an industrial city.

Tilburg (2013-06):

Tilburg is not the nicest city. The Loonse Dunes nearby are worth a walk when you are near. We camped in the Chaamse wouds.

Arnhem (2011-00):

My visits were mostly work related, the city does not really offer much of interest but is OK to walk through.

Enschede (2011-00):

My visits were mostly work related, with the nearby Twente University, the city does not really offer much of interest but it is still nice to walk through.
