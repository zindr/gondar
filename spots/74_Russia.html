Russia

Moscow (2012-10):

A few days on transit in the biggest European city... well yeah... it is interesting, people run instead of walk, it is expensive, there are a few nice museums, and of course, the red square, the Kremlin... these speak to the imagination. I loved it.

Sergiyev Posad (2012-10):

The most popular ring city with its Lavra, easily accessed from Moscow for a short visit, it shows a number of churches. The town is nothing of interest.
