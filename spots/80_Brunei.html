Brunei

Bandar Seri Bewagan (2013-05):

Even the tourist information officer in charge suggests that there is not really anything to do here... I think one of the mosques was downright amazing though, the other one was OK too. But the city sleeps.

Bangsar (2013-05):

A tiny regional capital, worth a visit by boat only for visiting a nearby hill with cloud forest. This was indicated as alternative to the crazy expensive national park.

Bukit Patoi (2013-05):

A hill hike, some cloud forest, an OK view and a centipede. Well, just to do something, why not.

Kuala Belait (2013-05):

A proof that also the rest of Brunei is completely boring, just here for starting my hitch-hiking out of the country.
