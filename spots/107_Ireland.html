Ireland

Dublin (2019-09):

Maybe the nicest about Dublin, is just hanging out in a pub with live folk music, maybe even having a bite, life irish stew.

Galway (2019-09):

A quick pass by, this small city looks nice for a day. From here on west is the connemara peninsula.

Connemare National Park (2019-09):

Very popular hike up the mountain, with a nice view on the bay and the hills.

Connamare road trip west (2019-09):

The peninsula is quite beautiful, swirling the narrow road near the coast. First stop, Errislannan.

Connamare road trip south (2019-09):

Following the coastline, enjoying the quite outdoors, a few stops at the beach, for example in Dogs Bay.

Corofin (2019-09):

This small town in the Burren that is the base to the national park is good for a beer, a meal or even a hostel.

Poulnabrone Dolmen (2019-09):

Very often photographed are the Dolmen, so ok, if you are around.

Cliffs of Moher (2019-09):

An Irish highlight, the cliffs, with very nice views on the steep shore. Steep are also the entrance fees though.

Killarney National Park (2019-09):

A nice enough hike up the hill brings you through forest and a small waterfall, and do not miss the nearby Gap of Dunloe. The town of Killarney is maybe a bit too much touristtown.

Kenmare (2019-09):

A small town, OK for a quick stopover on route to Beara peninsula.

Glengarriff Woods Nature Reserve (2019-09):

A small hike up in the forest, beautiful.

Healy Pass (2019-09):

Scenic pass cutting through the Beara peninsula.

Eyeries (2019-09):

Tidiest town, at least last year. Towns here are colorful, but small.

Allihies (2019-09):

Another small town, good to park near the beach, and have another Irish stew.

Kinsale (2019-09):

Road trips bring you to various places, also this small city which was good for a short walk through and a drink.

Cork (2019-09):

The second city, with a few nice streets, but maybe we could have skipped it. We camped in nearby Blarney.

Cobh (2019-09):

Again another small city on the road, good enough to have a quick stop and relax at the seafront.

Ballycotton Cliff Walk (2019-09):

Not the cliff walk in Moher, but if you are near and want to freshen your nose, this may be it.

Kilkenny (2019-09):

A city on a Friday evening, people are on the move, all ages, not necesserally sober. With a bit of luck you end up in the right pub.

Glendalough Park (2019-09):

A very popular park near Dublin, part of the Wicklow way. The hike is nice and some parts are scenic, with some deer to spot as well.

Sally Gap (2019-09):

The drive from Glendalough to Enniskerry covering the scenic Sally Pass was very nice.
