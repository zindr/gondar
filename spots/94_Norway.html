A first hike starts a bit down from Fredvang and goes to the other side accross the mountain trhough very boggy terrain. It gave us a first glimpse from up above over the Lofoten.

The shortest name possible for a town, a small (ex?) fishing community that seems to cater to tourists. It is were the road ends, so you might just stand theree gazing over the cliff.

Himmeltindan (2015-08):

For the views you may want to hike up here, and stay a bit at the beach afterwards to recover.

Hemmingsvaer (2015-08):

Tromso (2015-08):
