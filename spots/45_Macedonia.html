Macedonia

Skopje (2008-10):

While the center is charming, you are quickly out of the center and the rest is not really interesting. A large number of new ornamental bridges and statutes did change the grandeur of the city somewhat, as I noticed 10 years later.

Bitola (2014-06):

The charming city of Bitola is a perfect day trip, with a nice old part and for strolling the main avenue.

Ohrid (2014-06):

This lake side city is the most popular Macedonian tourist spot and can easily entertain you for a day. It is a world away from nearby Albania.

Mavrovo (2014-06):

This town is mostly a ski resort and offers very little, but it is one of the bases for visiting the Mavrovo national park. We hiked for 7 hours up to Jance.

Galichnik (2014-06):

Small town famous for cheese and an ending point for a hike from Mavrovo.

Janche (2014-06):

Small town with traditional houses at the end of a hike from Mavrovo, near the main road.

Debar (2014-06):
