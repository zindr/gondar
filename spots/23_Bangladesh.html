Bangladesh

Dhaka (2002-11):

Overcrowded, rough, poor and interesting, definitely worth a peak but it is clear why you do not see tourists. I was there in the middle of Ramadan but no particular problems with that.

Chittagong (2002-11):

I was only here briefly, with its curious and friendly people and a nice and vivid late night market.

Rangamati (2002-11):

I was also here only shortly, tourists were not allowed to be on their own following a kidnapping a few years before. But people there treated me again very nice and I had some interesting time up there in Rangamati.

Mongla (2002-11):

Gateway to the Sundarbans national park, this tiny little town is just that.
