Czech Republic

Prague (2000-09):

The center is really beautiful, but also overly crowded.

Kutna Hora (2000-09):

The first time ending up here on a Sunday without preparation, it was just another side to Czechia. The second time, I had a better look, the bone church was ok, the gothic church too.

Olomouc (2010-05):

This student city is actually quite nice, with a museum and some nice bars to hang out.

Cesky Krumlov (2021-07):

The most recommended alternative to prague, this small city is realy nice. You will find many locals with a kano on the water, often with navy t-shirt.

Telc (2021-07):
