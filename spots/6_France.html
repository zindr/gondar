France

Paris (1992-00):

Famous Paris is the city that shows off, in fact it is maybe the series of ex-presidents that show off instead with the various famous buildings. I recommend Musee d`Orsay.

Versailles (1992-00):

The city of Versailles is mostly known for its palace, home of Louis XIV. Friends of mine lived nearby.

Mont Saint Michel (1998-05):

One of the most famous landmarks in France, Mont Saint Michel is quite imposing.

Saint Brieuc (1998-05):

My visit was part of a wedding, it is a tourist area but not the most popular one.

Nice (1993-08):

My first independent trip (1993), with my girlfriend, was in the south of France, staring in Nice. It was fun, but I would not recommend it to anyway.

Cannes (1993-08):

A brief visit to Cannes was OK, but nothing of real interest.

Nimes (1993-08):

Nimes is just a city in the south of France, nothing of particular interest.

Castillon-du-Gard (1991-08):

I visited this small town as part of a youth vacation, a rural area with a historic bridge.
