Croatia

Zagreb (2014-06):

Zagreb has a great vibe to it, reminding me a bit of Berlin albeit being much smaller.

Zadar (2014-06):

This cheap flight destination is quite nice for starting a trip. A relaxed city with a few tourist sites that can keep you going for a day.

Plitvice Lakes (2014-06):

It is seemingly the number one tourist site of the country, half a day of nice walks up and around beautiful lakes covered by tourist hurdles for a steep 15 Euro entry ticket.

Karlobag (2014-06):

This is just a small, quit touristy, place on the beautiful coast of Croatia.

Gospic (2014-06):

Getting stuck hitch-hiking... this is just a city on route.

Dubrovnik (2014-06):

Maybe the best tourist city in all of the Balkan, Dubrovnik has an imposing fort swamped by tourists and tourist industry. It is good for hanging out for a day, I guess.
