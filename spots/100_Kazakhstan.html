Kazakhstan

Almaty (2016-09):

The number one city in the country and the region, cultural capital, provides easy access to bars and mountains.

Ile Alatau NP (2016-09):

The mountains are OK for a hike, but Kyrgyzstan and maybe the north east of Kazakhstan offer better hikes.

Shymkent (2016-09):

Much less Russian style, this Uzbek city is kinda nice for a stroll.

Turkestan (2016-09):

The one world class historical site, although not rivaling the ones from Uzbekistan, is the only reason to come here.

Karagandy (2016-09):

No reason to come here, but you maybe get stuck here. It is the hub to go east, north and south.

Karkaraly NP (2016-09):

OK hiking here in Karkaraly national park, although the forests may seem a bit too normal for western Europeans.

Astana (2016-09):

The new capital, and the architectural showpiece, at least in the center part.
