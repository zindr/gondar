Chile

Santiago (2012-01):

A quick stopover for a day, just to have a taste of the Chile big city life. It was OK, I liked it.

Puerto Natales (2011-12):

Gateway to Torres Del Paine, a charming city with wooden houses and very fine landscapes.

Torres Del Paine (2011-12):

Hiking and hiking, in brilliant diverse scenery for about a few days to over a week... I loved it, superb, do not miss it when near.

Cerro Castillo (2011-12):

A border crossing in the middle of nowhere, not a place to linger around.

Chile Chico (2012-01):

The border town, where you can try your luck to get out. Transportation is not very well developed in this region.

Puerto Ibanez (2012-01):

Small small town, trying to get out, again... all buses were full, so... hitch-hiking again.

Coyhaique (2012-01):

Nothing to do here... but finally I reached a city, just trying to get transport to the next one. Public transport here is a disaster.

Puerto Montt (2012-01):

A tourist city, and a hub for the areas around.

Ancud (2012-01):

Transit, just passing through, but seemingly also an OK town.

Castro (2012-01):

A nice main city on the island Chiloe, good for a relaxed day or maybe two.

Punihil (2012-01):

Watching the penguins with a little boat trip, funny little buggers.

Arica (2012-01):

Chilean border town, in transit from Peru to Bolivia, it is not too bad to get stuck here.

Patacamaya (2012-01):

Transit from Arica in Chile to Oruro in Bolivia, a really cool place, up high (> 4600 m.), really nice trip.
