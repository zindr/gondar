Cyprus

Pafos (2022-11):

A little city, with an airport, and some historical remnants that we did not visit.

Polis (2022-11):

A tiny city in the far corner, with some nice hiking nearby on the Akamas peninsula, like the Afrodite.

Avakas (2022-11):

There is a gorge to do some hikign too, and you can turn back to visit Lara beach nearby.

Troodos (2022-11):

A bit of hiking is possible in the Troodos mountains, we did the Atalante. The road up there from the west is endless.

Tseri (2022-11):

Just because there was a hostel, no reason to get here in this town.

Greek Nicosia (2022-11):

The Greek side of the capital was worth a day of strolling. Weird to see it split up like this.

Turkish Nicosia (2022-11):

The Turkish side of the capital was worth a day of strolling too. Weird to see it split up like this.

Girne (2022-11):

Girne boasts a beautiful harbor, and nice fortress, but because we could not drive our rental we did not visit much more in Kyrenia.

Famagusta (2022-11):

Famagusta has a very nice old town, and here you can visit part of its ghost town remainings after the 74 war.

Ayia Napa (2022-11):

Known for its coast line, we went to have a look.

Larnaka (2022-11):

A vibrant beach city, lining up restaurants. You may come across a flamingo in the nearby lake.

Limasol (2022-11):

A big city considering Cyprus, packed with restaurants near the harbor.
