Senegal

St. Louis (2007-12):

French colonialist atmosphere, it was surely nice to hang out for a day. It is big enough to get entertained for those interested while still not too much of a city.

Touba (2007-12):

My experience in Touba is a bit atypical because of Tabaski I was invited to stay with a family, one man and two woman. The city is nice at first sight, but most of the time I spent in peoples houses, so I did not see that much of it.

Kaolack (2007-12):

It is not a place you want to go to, but you may well pass it a few times when around. You might see only its dirty, chaotic and polluted bus station.

Tambacounda (2007-12):

On route to Mali, a brief stop... the road up there was beautiful, but difficult. African travel you know.

Dakar (2018-11):

Missed it last time in Senegal, this is a true African city on the waterfront. Expensive Almadie differs from downtown plateau, slightly more organized Colobane and suburbs like Keur Massar.

Toubakouta (2018-11):

Take a boat trip in the mangroves and have a relaxing walk near time when around here.

Popenguine (2018-11):

Known for pilgrimage, it was a good place to cut our trip north to Dakar and sit our ass in the sea.

Ziguinchor (2018-11):

Nice capital city of the southern Casamance, I should maybe have spent a bit more time there.

Oussouye (2018-11):

Tiny regional city which serves as a good base to reach nearby areas.

Cap Skirring (2018-11):

Known for its beaches, a place for tourists with many more amenities then most nearby cities.

Diembereng (2018-11):

Small and scenic little village, fun to walk through and get to the beach.

Kachiouane (2018-11):

Tiny village that is now attracting many more tourists, I loved the walk all the way up there.

Saly (2018-11):

The camping place in between Saly and Mbour gave easy access to both, Saly itself is for tourists not like me.

Lac Rose (2018-11):

Sometimes it is pink, depends on the light, not when I was there, luckily the walk up to the beach was nice and they were playing some techno there.
