Senegal

St. Louis (2007-12):

French colonialist atmosphere, it was surely nice to hang out for a day. It is big enough to get entertained for those interested while still not too much of a city.

Touba (2007-12):

My experience in Touba is a bit atypical because of Tabaski I was invited to stay with a family, one man and two woman. The city is nice at first sight, but most of the time I spent in peoples houses, so I did not see that much of it.

Tambacounda (2007-12):

On route to Mali, a brief stop... the road up there was beautiful, but difficult. African travel you know.
