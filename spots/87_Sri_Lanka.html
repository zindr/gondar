Sri Lanka

Colombo (2014-03):

This is the kinda city all tourist say it is not worth a visit, but the alternative Negombo has become just that. I thought Colombo was ok for a day.

Haputale (2014-03):

A nice hill city in tea country, a good place to start some walks. The scenery is nice and the people still OK.

Tea Country Hike (2014-03):

A hike from up to the viewpoint (not really interesting) and further all the way to Bandarawella was very nice.

Nuwara Ellya (2014-03):

Little England, well, true... good enough for a few hours just passing through.

Jaffna (2014-03):

A city, a real one with a bit a rougher edge and nice. I like this stuff, why, I do not know myself.

Point Pedro (2014-03):

Quiet place, at the northern end. It is good for only a few hours, but an easy side trip from Jaffna. 

Pollonuwara (2014-03):

Temple complex from earlier times, but like all tourist attractions in Sri Lanka, ridiculously expensive. I skipped all other expensive sites and attractions.

Trinco (2014-03):

The city in the north east, a base for beaching I guess. The city is OK and the people nice. The beaches are not so amazing.

Uppuveli Beach (2014-03):

OK for half an hour, this beach that comes recommended. I think it is a bit overrated but at least there are not too many tourists.

Nilaveli Beach (2014-03):

Another beach spot, with at the time almost no tourists. So, OK for an hour of sitting in the sand.

Galle (2014-03):

Visit the Dutch fort around here... it will keep you busy for half a day. The area has it fair share of tourists already.

Unawatuna (2014-03):

Popular beach place, with some white sand beaches and restaurants.
