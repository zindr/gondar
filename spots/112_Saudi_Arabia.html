Saudi Arabia

Riyadh (2023-11):

A slightly interesting Dehriya (At-Turaif World Heritage) and Masmak make this pedestrian unfriendly environment not totally tourist unfriendly, and you can go out to the end of the world if your buget allows.

Jeddah (2023-11):

A moderatly interesting old town make this otherwise pedestrian unfriendly environment OK, you may also visit the world tallest fountain at the corniche.

Taif (2023-11):

This hill escape from the heat in Jeddah is popular with locals, it should be fine but I did not spent much time in the city as I was hijacked in a friendly way by locals. The nearby Ash Sharaf hills were OK for a visit.

Abha (2023-11):

This other hill town boasts a good hike down the highest mountain, jebel sawda, and a visit to nearby and very nice historical town Rijal Alma.

Yanbu (2023-11):

The diving place, with its walkable streets and nice parks and old town, was my favorite city in the country.

Medina (2023-11):

The burial place of Mohammed, it has a few nice spots, and if you can enter the fenced part of the mosque, it is nice and interesting.

Al Ula (2023-11):

Famous for the Madain Saleh, with Nabatean tombs, it is especially beautiful for its scenery. It has an open air museum too.

Tabuk (2023-11):

Probably much more of interest in the near future, as part of the Neom project, now it does not show much of interest.
