Togo

Lome (2015-03):

The capital of Togo is quite nice to spend a few days, it is said to have a good night life. It is by far the biggest city you will find in Togo.

Vapozo (2015-03):

The beaches nearby Lome are here, with Chez Alice as the place for camping and some bars to have a beer too.

Kpalime (2015-03):

Said to be the nicest of cities in Togo, Kpalime is in mountainous region and equipped to deal with tourists. If you like a hike in the forest for visiting a waterfall, this may be your place. Ktoulo village is the starting point for some hikes.

Togoville (2015-03):

The old dilapidated church that the pope visited because Mary showed herself may not that good a reason to visit Togoville, still not sure what would be.

Aneho (2015-03):

At the border with Benin you could hang out a few hours in Aneho, an OK place but nothing special. The market has some fetish articles but these you can find in many places.

Kara (2015-03):

Kara is just a city up north, not too interesting but OK for a stop on the way to the Tamberma region.

Kande (2015-03):

Kande is a small town that you will just pass on the way to the Tamberma region.

Koutammakou (2015-03):

The UNESCO region where the Tamberma have there fortress-like houses.
