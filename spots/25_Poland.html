Poland

Warsaw (2013-08):

It is not as ugly as they say, at least a small part is really nice. It is also a fun place to go and have a beer, or lazy about in one of the nice parks.

Wroclaw (2013-08):

Student town Wroclaw is nice to hang out for a day or maybe two, but it lacks tourist attractions.

Krakow (2004-08):

The old capital and most beautiful city of the country, it is a favorite. I thought parts of the Wawel castle was worth a visit, although a bit expensive.

Auswitsch (2004-08):

If you are near you should visit this reminder of one of the darkest periods in our history, the concentration camps of Auswitsch and Birkenau.

Wieliczka (2013-08):

The salt mines are very expensive and look a bit like a theme park. It was a tiny bit impressive, but I would not recommend it.

Zakopane (High Tatras) (2013-08):

At the foot of the Tatras mountain range, you visit it if you like hiking. The hikes are quite nice.
