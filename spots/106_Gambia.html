Gambia

Banjul (2018-11):

Often skipped, this small capital, but worth passing through to get a glimpse of the chaos.

Sukuta (2018-11):

Just a city with some nearby tourist attractions, like Abuko forest national park, the beach and Bijelo.

Abuko (2018-11):

A small national park in the middle of the city, almost, good for a stroll.

Marakissa (2018-11):

Nice scenery for a walk in the high grass, see some birds, just a relaxed place.

Soma (2018-11):

Get in and get out kind of place, where you take the ferry north.

Janjanbureh (2018-11):

The main city in the east of The Gambia, a good base for some small walks around and maybe see some birds and monkeys.

Farafenni (2018-11):

Bordertown hub, just a stopover.
