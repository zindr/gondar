Gondar's Way highlights visited countries up to now. Traveling began many years ago and is done in chunks of a few weeks up to four months. After my first few trips I decided to select countries to make a chain from east to west and when it finished from south to north. Now I am filling in the gaps.

For a visual: Google World Map. Details at The Trail.
For a keeping track: My Blogspot. Details at The Story.

I do budget overlanding. Increasingly I pushed myself out of the comfort bubble which resulted in some really extraordinary experiences. The goal now is to be amazed from time to time, to get to the point where it simply does not make sense. Personal highlights often involve exhausting public transport, extreme hospitality, weird stuff on your plate, ...
